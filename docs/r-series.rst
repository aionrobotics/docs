======================
R Series Documentation
======================

The R series represents Aion Robotics smallest weight class platforms.

- The R1 is 4x4 skid-steer chassis designed for educational, and professional research & development applications.

- The R6 is an heavily upgraded, light commercial weatherproof chassis designed with custom integrations in mind.
